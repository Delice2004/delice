About AgriCow Connect

Improving livestock health and agriculture in rural communities

Who We Are

AgriCow Connect is a community-driven digital platform that empowers farmers and cattle owners by connecting them with professional veterinarians and agricultural experts. Founded to bridge the gap in livestock care and crop advisory services, our mission is to bring better farming solutions to rural communities through accessible technology.

Our Mission

To support rural farmers with reliable, fast, and affordable access to animal healthcare and agricultural expertise, improving both livestock productivity and community well-being.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital agricultural support network in Africa, trusted by farmers and experts alike for transforming rural agriculture.
